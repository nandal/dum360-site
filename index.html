How it works

From idle device to national compute

Enroll once, set your hours, and let DUM360 safely put your spare cycles to work.

1

Install & verify

A lightweight, sandboxed node daemon (K3s/KubeEdge) or mobile WASM client. Hardware integrity checked via Secure Enclave.

2

Pre-inform availability

Book recurring windows — e.g. 11 PM–7 AM. The orchestrator maps workloads 6 hours in advance.

3

Compute safely

Encrypted, confidential containers run jobs you can't read. Tasks mirror across ≥3 machines for resilience.

4

Get paid in UPI

Usage is metered to the rupee and paid straight to your VPA. Graceful drain returns your device 30 min before exit.
